Core mechanic

THREE MOVES.
INFINITE DEPTH.

🔄
Action 1
Reload
+1 ammo (max 3). Hit the beat at the perfect 0.25s window for +2. No ammo = no offense. Every reload is a read on your opponent.
🔫
Action 2
Shoot
Spend 1 ammo, deal 1 damage. Perfect timing makes it harder to block. Read the opponent's pattern. Punish the reload.
🛡️
Action 3
Block
Deflects any standard shot. Time it perfectly and 1 damage reflects straight back. The most skill-expressive move in the game.
🚀
Signature move
Bazooka
Reload three times in a row. Your next shot is unblockable and deals 2 damage. Your opponent can see it loading. They still can't stop it.
