Core mechanic

THREE MOVES.
INFINITE DEPTH.

🔄

Reload

Gain +1 ammo (max 3). Hit the beat perfectly for +2. No ammo — no offense. Choose your moment.

🔫

Shoot

Spend 1 ammo to deal 1 damage. Read your opponent. Punish a reload. Never waste ammo on a block.

🛡️

Block

Deflect any incoming shot. Time it perfectly on the beat — reflect 1 damage straight back.
